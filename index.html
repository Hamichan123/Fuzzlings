Good to know

Questions, answered

Fuzzlings are a collection of 2,222 hand-drawn fluffy, one-eyed monsters living on Ethereum. Each one is unique, with its own colours and traits.

The mint is completely free for whitelisted wallets. You only ever cover the standard Ethereum network gas fee.

Head to the whitelist page and complete four tasks: follow our X account, like & retweet the pinned post, comment and drop your comment link, then submit your EVM wallet.

Any EVM-compatible wallet works — MetaMask, Rainbow, Coinbase Wallet and others. Just make sure it's an Ethereum address starting with 0x.
